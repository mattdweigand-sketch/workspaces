# Platform Boundary

## Platform Owns

- Document storage.
- Access control.
- Version history.
- Audit trail.
- System-of-record facts.

## Kit Owns

- Source authority interpretation.
- Conflict and gap visibility.
- Evidence support mapping.
- Question list for human resolution.

## Human Approval

A human resolves conflicts and approves any claim that will be used in a decision or stakeholder communication.
