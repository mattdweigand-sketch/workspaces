## Authority Ladder

- Authoritative: source of record, executed agreement, approved report, audited figure, final decision.
- Supporting: analyst note, internal summary, working model, meeting note.
- Background: market research, commentary, general context.
- Superseded: old version, draft replaced by final, stale report.
