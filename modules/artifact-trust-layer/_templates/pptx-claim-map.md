## Deck Review Rules

- Every chart must trace to a source, workbook, or source-of-record export.
- Speaker notes may explain evidence. They may not introduce unsupported facts.
- Slide titles that state conclusions count as claims.
- Old deck slides are not authoritative unless the source packet says they are.
- Mixed-period comparisons require source dates for each figure.
