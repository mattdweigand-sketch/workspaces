# Artifact Spec: Atlas Growth IC Deck

## Artifact

- Name: Atlas Growth IC deck
- Type: Deck
- Audience: Investment committee
- Decision or communication job: Support a proceed or pause decision.
- Human approver: Deal lead
- Delivery platform: Approval workflow

## Inputs

- Source packet: `../diligence-evidence-map/sample-source-packet.md`
- Claim evidence map: `../diligence-evidence-map/sample-claim-evidence-map.md`
- Source of record for figures: Customer export and approved model
- Deterministic check owner: Finance owner

## Required Sections

| Section | Purpose | Required Claims Or Figures |
|---|---|---|
| Investment thesis | State why the deal may be attractive. | Customer count, retention, growth drivers |
| Key risks | Make the downside explicit. | Retention conflict, market leadership support gap |
| Recommendation | Frame decision options. | Proceed, pause, or request more diligence |

| Claim Or Topic | Reason |
|---|---|
| Atlas is the category leader. | Unsupported by current source packet. |

## Review Gates
