# Human Approval

Artifact Trust Layer review ends with a human decision.

Workspces can make evidence, assumptions, source freshness, formula risk, and unresolved issues visible. It cannot approve the artifact for external use or decision use.

## Approval States

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `approved` | The named approver accepts the artifact for the stated use. |
| `approved with conditions` | The artifact can move forward only if listed conditions are met. |
| `not approved` | The artifact should not be used until revised and reviewed again. |

## Approver Responsibilities

- Resolve unsupported claims.
- Decide whether assumptions are acceptable.
- Confirm sensitive wording.
- Approve commitments.
- Confirm the artifact's intended use.
- Decide whether remaining warnings are acceptable.

## What Approval Does Not Mean

Approval does not certify source systems, calculations, formulas, permissions, delivery, or audit trail. Those stay with the platform or deterministic owner.
