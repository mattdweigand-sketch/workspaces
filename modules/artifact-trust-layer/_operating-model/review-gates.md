# Review Gates

Use these gates when an artifact will support a decision, stakeholder communication, diligence finding, or investor-facing narrative.

## Gate 1: Source Packet

Pass when:

- Every source has an ID.
- Authority is classified.
- Stale or superseded sources are visible.
- Source-of-record questions are listed.

## Gate 2: Claim Evidence Map

Pass when:

- Load-bearing claims are mapped.
- Numbers and dates have source IDs.
- Assumptions and interpretations are labeled.
- Unsupported claims are blocked or assigned to an owner.

## Gate 3: Artifact-Specific Review

Pass when:

- Deck claims, workbook controls, or document claims have been reviewed.
- Stale numbers are flagged.
- Formula or chart risks are visible.
- Sensitive commitments have an owner.

## Gate 4: Human Approval

Pass when:

- The approver is named.
- Conditions are listed.
- Open issues are accepted, resolved, or blocked.
- The approved use is explicit.
