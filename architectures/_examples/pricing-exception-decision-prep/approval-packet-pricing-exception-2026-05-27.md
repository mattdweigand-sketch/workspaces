# Approval Packet: Pricing Exception

## Decision

Approve or reject a 12% non-standard discount for the Northwind renewal.

## Options

- Approve the full discount with a one-year term.
- Approve 8% with a two-year commitment.
- Reject discount and hold standard pricing.

## Tradeoffs

The full discount may preserve renewal speed but creates precedent. The two-year option protects value but may slow close timing.

## Challenge

The request assumes price is the only blocker. Evidence is incomplete. Customer success notes mention adoption concerns that may not be solved by discounting.

## Recommendation

Approve only if the account owner confirms adoption concerns are resolved and the pricing model owner confirms margin impact.
