# Before You Trust This

The single place that collects every value in this workspace the team has NOT yet confirmed. A
new owner reads it first and clears it before anything board-facing or decision-bearing ships.

In this worked example the rows are shown **CLEARED**, because the example depicts a finished,
distributed report. In a real workspace, these start as `[NEEDS CONFIRMATION]` and a named owner
clears them.

## Status: CLEARED (worked example)

| Value / field | Where it lives | Why it is high-stakes | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Confidentiality / forward-looking footer | `_config/constraints.md` | legal disclosure language | CFO + Counsel | CLEARED 2026-01-20 |
| Forward-looking-statement rules | `_config/constraints.md` | governance/liability surface | CFO + Counsel | CLEARED 2026-01-20 |
| The metrics in the pack (ARR, NRR, burn, cash, runway, headcount) | `01_data/output/metrics-pack.md` | the board acts on these | CFO (close owner) | CLEARED 2026-06-02 |

Note: the metrics themselves come from the company's system of record at month-end close — the
platform — not from this workspace. The data stage verifies and reconciles them into the pack;
the draft never originates or recomputes a figure.
