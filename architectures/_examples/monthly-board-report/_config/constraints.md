# Constraints — Northwind Analytics board reporting

The never-do list and the required-always list for board communications. L3 reference, loaded in
stages 02 and 03. Confirmed with the CFO and outside counsel on 2026-01-20. Re-confirm annually.

## Required on every board report

- **Confidentiality and forward-looking footer** (verbatim): "This report is confidential and
  prepared solely for the board of directors of Northwind Analytics. It contains forward-looking
  statements that reflect current expectations and are subject to risks and uncertainties; actual
  results may differ materially."
- **As-of date** on all metrics (the month-end close date).
- **Source basis line:** state that figures are from the company's system of record at month-end
  close.

## Hard Rules (never violate)

1. No figure that is not tied to the verified metrics pack. Every number traces to
   `01_data/output/metrics-pack.md`.
2. No em dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
3. No guarantees or unqualified projections. Forward-looking statements stay qualified and
   grounded ("we expect," not "we will").
4. No omission of the confidentiality/forward-looking footer or of any preliminary/unaudited
   label carried from the metrics pack.
5. No significance inflation. No "exceptional," "outstanding," "transformative" about results
   unless the numbers plainly support it and the CEO allows it.
6. No defensiveness on a miss. State it, state the action, move on.

## Style Rules

7. Lead with the result. No throat-clearing opener.
8. Sentences average 12 to 18 words.
9. State numbers plainly; let the data carry the message.
10. Address the board's most likely concern directly rather than hoping they miss it.
