# Before You Trust This

The single place that collects every value in this workspace the team has NOT yet confirmed. A
new owner reads it first and clears it before any candidate-facing or decision-bearing output
ships.

In this worked example the rows are shown **CLEARED**, because the example depicts a finished,
validated cycle. In a real workspace, these start as `[NEEDS CONFIRMATION]` and a named owner
clears them.

## Status: CLEARED (worked example)

| Value / field | Where it lives | Why it is high-stakes | Owner | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| The hiring bar (core-dimension rule, disqualifiers) | `_config/thresholds.md` | decides who gets an offer | Hiring manager + recruiting lead | CLEARED 2026-04-01 |
| The role scorecard and level definition | `_config/decision-criteria.md` | defines what "senior" means here | Hiring manager | CLEARED 2026-04-01 |
| Comp band for the level | (HRIS, not in this workspace) | over-leveled offer risk | Recruiting lead + Finance | CLEARED 2026-04-01 |
| Work-authorization / sponsorship rules | `_config/thresholds.md` | legal/requirement disqualifier | Recruiting ops + Legal | CLEARED 2026-04-01 |

Note: candidate facts (resume, application, interview scheduling, status) live in the ATS, the
platform of record — not in this workspace. This workspace snapshots and reasons over them; it
never originates them.
