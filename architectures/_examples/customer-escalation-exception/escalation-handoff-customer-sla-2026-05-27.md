# Escalation Handoff: Customer SLA Exception

## Exception

Customer requests weekend coverage outside the contracted SLA because a production launch depends on resolution.

## Rule Context

Standard SLA does not include weekend support. Contract allows paid emergency support with approval.

## Options

- Offer paid emergency support with support leader approval.
- Decline weekend coverage and provide Monday priority handling.
- Escalate to account owner for commercial concession decision.

## Recommended Handoff

Escalate to support leader and account owner. Do not commit until approval is recorded in the support platform.
