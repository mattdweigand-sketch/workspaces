# Stage 01: Decision Frame

## Purpose

Define the decision before analyzing it.

## Inputs

- Request or proposal.
- Verified facts from platform or owner.
- `_config/decision-criteria.md`
- `_config/platform-boundary.md`

## Process

1. State the decision in one sentence.
2. Identify the decision owner and deadline.
3. Separate verified facts from assumptions.
4. List constraints, required approvals, and platform records.
5. Identify what would make the decision reversible or irreversible.

## Output

Write to `01_decision_frame/output/decision-frame-[name]-[date].md`.

## Done Looks Like

Everyone can tell what decision is being made, by whom, and on what facts.
